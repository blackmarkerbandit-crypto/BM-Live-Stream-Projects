Intro Screen + VO

Real Talk Segment
Trending emotional/spiritual/life topic — relationships, healing, intuition, burnout, energy shifts. Personal stories, humor, audience reflection questions. Cam A main, Cam B cutaways.
Real Talk Stinger
8:00 – 15:00
Live Call-In Q&A
First callers enter queue. Quick tarot pulls or intuitive insights. Life, love, career, purpose, timing, healing questions. Fast-paced and emotionally engaging. Display call-in number on lower third: 515-JAESHOW.
Call-In Stinger
15:00 – 20:00
Card of the Night
Pull one tarot card live. Explain symbolism, lesson, energetic meaning. Tie to current astrology and audience emotions. Ask viewers to comment how card resonates. Cam C overhead for card pull.
Card of the Night Stinger
20:00 – 32:00
Horoscopes — All 12 Signs
~1 min per sign. Love, money, healing, intuition themes. Order: Aries → Taurus → Gemini → Cancer → Leo → Virgo → Libra → Scorpio → Sagittarius → Capricorn → Aquarius → Pisces. Cam A, zodiac lower thirds in vMix.
Horoscopes Stinger
32:00 – 40:00
Deeper Live Readings
Select 2–3 audience members for deeper readings. Mix tarot, intuition, astrology, pendulum, or mediumship. Focus on transformation and empowerment. Cam A + Cam C overhead as needed.
Readings Stinger
40:00 – 48:00
Hot Topic / Spiritual Teaching
Teach a spiritual concept in a grounded, relatable way. Examples: claircognizance, intuition opening, energy vampires & boundaries, manifestation myths. Cam A primary, Cam B cinematic cuts.
Hot Topic Stinger
48:00 – 55:00
Rapid Fire Questions
Fast audience questions. Pull one card per question. Keep energy upbeat and entertaining. Cam A tight close-up. High energy pace.
Rapid Fire Stinger
55:00 – 60:00
Closing & Outro
Recap the night's energy. Thank viewers and callers. Mention upcoming shows and website. Invite social follows. End with signature outro tagline + VO. Outro screen overlay in vMix.
