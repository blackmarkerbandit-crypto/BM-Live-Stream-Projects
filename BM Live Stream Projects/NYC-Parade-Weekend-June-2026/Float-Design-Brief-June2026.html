2 — Theme Hierarchy

BG

Background / Overarching: "Somos Más Que 100x35"

The official 2026 NPRDP parade theme. Use their provided creative as an ambient, textural backdrop that sets cultural context and ties us into the larger parade story. It lives behind our float theme — not competing with it.

Theme file: FLOAT DESIGN FILES / PR PARADE THEME / SomosMasQue100x35.png

FG

Foreground / Our Theme: "BlackMarker.TV — Celebrating Puerto Rican Pioneers"

Our identity and message live in the foreground. BlackMarker.TV is the primary brand. "Celebrating Puerto Rican Pioneers" is the headline. The parade theme supports us — we don't disappear into it.

The two themes should feel unified — the parade theme gives cultural depth, our theme gives it specificity and a face. Neither should fight the other.

9 — NPRDP Compliance Notes

Rules to Keep in Mind
  • Rule 8: Puerto Rican flag displayed with dignity — no alterations, no caricatures, no distortions of any kind.
  • Rule 11c: BMM/BlackMarker.TV logo is cleared by NPRDP rep ✓ — community/cultural branding is OK. Do not add any other major brand sponsor logos that haven't been pre-approved.
  • Rule 15e: Confirm DJ booth and performer layout with Bond Floats — must respect upper deck capacity limits.
  • Rule 13c: Audio: max 85dB. Silence sound effects at 51st St / St. Patrick's Cathedral. No sirens, horns, or foghorn effects near the TV broadcast stand.
  • All partner language: Community Partners only. No "sponsor," "presented by," or financial arrangement language anywhere on the float.
