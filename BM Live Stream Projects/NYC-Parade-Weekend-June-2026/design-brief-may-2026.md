### Theme

**"Somos Más Que 100x35"** is the 2026 NPRDP parade theme. Puerto Rico measures 100 miles × 35 miles — the phrase declares that Puerto Ricans are more than the island's geography. It's a celebration of the diaspora, the culture that lives everywhere Puerto Ricans are.

Our contingent angle bridges directly into this: **"Celebrating Puerto Rican Pioneers"** — honoring pioneers in music, culture, and entertainment through talent Joselito Da Puppet, DJ Charlie Chase, and Thirstin Howl the 3rd.

**Primary palette confirmed ✓** — use these hex codes across ALL materials (float, shirts, postcards, stream graphics, billboard):

| Color | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Red | `#CF142B` | Puerto Rican flag red — primary |
| Blue | `#0038A8` | Puerto Rican flag blue — secondary |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Type, backgrounds |
| Black | `#000000` | Type, outlines |
| Yellow / Gold | `#F5C800` | Accents, parade theme highlights |

> BMM brand uses `#E8181C` (close match to flag red) — acceptable for digital. Use `#CF142B` for print and float graphics to stay true to the PR flag.

**Visual cues from official theme artwork to incorporate:** vejigante mask, bomba drum, compass star, Puerto Rico island silhouette, feather quill, mirror ball.
