## EOS At a Glance

**The Three Outcomes:** Vision + Traction + Healthy  
**The Six Components:** Vision — People — Data — Issues — Process — Traction  
**The Core Tools:** V/TO, Accountability Chart, Scorecard, Level 10 Meeting, Rocks, People Analyzer, IDS  
**The Two Leadership Roles:** Visionary (where are we going?) + Integrator (how do we get there?)  
**The Primary Book:** *Traction* by Gino Wickman  
**Timeline to Mastery:** 18–24 months  
**Ideal Company Size:** 10–250 employees  
**Adoption:** 280,000+ businesses worldwide
