## PHASE B — FIRST 30 DAYS
*Light spend. Focus on converting warm relationships.*

---

### B1 — Land First 3 Build Retainer Clients
**Deadline:** Day 30  
**Owner:** BMB  
**Budget:** $500–$1,000 (CRM tool, proposal software, or basic outreach)  

**Process:**
1. List 10 warm contacts — anyone who has paid BMB, referred BMB, or expressed interest
2. Send a personal (not bulk) message to each: *"I wanted to reach out personally — we've put together a streaming + marketing package and I think it's a fit for what you're doing."*
3. Offer a 30-minute call
4. Quote from the Build package tiers

**Target clients:**
- Independent event promoters with recurring events
- Music venues wanting an online presence
- Podcasters or show hosts who need a platform
- Local sports organizations
- Any business running regular online content

**Revenue target:** 3 clients × $1,500–$2,000/month = **$4,500–$6,000 MRR**  
**Annual value:** **$54,000–$72,000 ARR added**

**Success metric:** 3 signed retainer agreements by Day 30.

---

### B2 — Identify and Pitch 5 Venue Produce Retainer Targets
**Deadline:** Day 30 (identified) | Day 60 (first 2 signed)  
**Owner:** BMB  
**Budget:** $0 (outreach only at this stage)  

**Target venue types:**
- Churches / ministries doing weekly services
- Youth or adult sports leagues
- Music venues with weekly/bi-weekly shows
- Fitness studios doing live classes
- Recurring community or cultural events

**Retainer pitch:**
*"You're already booking us per-event. For a flat monthly rate you get guaranteed crew availability, priority scheduling, and consistent quality — and you simplify your billing."*

**Show the math to the client:**
- Current: 3 events × $800 = $2,400/month
- Retainer: $2,000/month for 4 slots + priority access = they save money, you get stability

**Revenue target:** 5 retainers × $2,000–$3,000/month = **$10,000–$15,000 MRR**  
**Annual value:** **$120,000–$180,000 ARR added**

**Success metric:** 5 venues identified and pitched by Day 30. First 2 signed by Day 60.

---

### B3 — Lock BlackMarker.tv Anchor Programming
**Deadline:** Day 30  
**Owner:** BMB  
**Budget:** Minimal (time and commitment)  

**Deliverable:** 2–3 shows with fixed schedules that will run every week without exception.

**Criteria for anchor shows:**
- Can be produced consistently with current resources
- Has a committed, reliable host
- Serves a specific, identifiable audience
- Differentiates from mainstream content

**The rule:** A show that runs every Thursday at 8pm for 6 months builds an audience. A show that runs when it's convenient does not. Consistency IS the product at this stage.

**Success metric:** 2–3 shows scheduled, committed, and running by Day 30. Calendar published publicly.

## PHASE C — FIRST 90 DAYS
*Capital deployment. Infrastructure and audience growth.*

---

### C1 — Upgrade Production Equipment
**Deadline:** Day 60  
**Budget:** $5,000–$10,000  

**Priority purchases (own instead of rent):**
- Encoding hardware or upgraded encoder software
- Camera upgrade if current gear is limiting quality
- Audio — the single most noticed quality indicator
- Backup internet connection (cellular bonding or 4G failover) for venue work

**Why now:** With 5+ venue retainers and ongoing Studio Enterprise work, owned gear pays back in 3–6 months through eliminated rental costs. Every job after payback is higher margin.

**Success metric:** Core equipment owned. Rental costs reduced by 80%+.

---

### C2 — Upgrade BlackMarker.tv Infrastructure
**Deadline:** Day 60  
**Budget:** $5,000–$10,000  

**Priority improvements:**
- Reliable, high-quality streaming delivery
- Clean, professional viewer interface
- Functional subscription payment flow (monthly + annual options)
- Functional PPV purchase flow (single event tickets)
- Mobile-friendly viewing experience
- Email capture on every page and stream

**Why this order:** Marketing spend before infrastructure is fixed is wasted money. Fix the conversion plumbing first.

**Success metric:** Platform can accept subscriptions, sell PPV, and deliver a reliable viewing experience before any paid marketing begins.

---

### C3 — Launch PPV Marketing Funnel
**Deadline:** Day 75–90  
**Budget:** $3,000–$5,000  

**The funnel (in sequence):**
1. Consistent anchor shows build weekly returning viewership
2. Email/SMS capture converts viewers to a direct contact list
3. PPV events are marketed to the list — not cold audiences
4. Social clips from shows drive new viewer discovery

**The math once the funnel exists:**
- 2,000 person email list × 10% conversion × $15 PPV = **$3,000 from one email**
- 5,000 person list × 10% × $15 = **$7,500 from one email**

**Success metric:** Email list of 500+ before first funded PPV event. First PPV event with this funnel generates 50+ buyers.

---

### C4 — First Brand Sponsorship Conversations
**Deadline:** Day 90  
**Budget:** $1,000–$2,000 (pitch deck, outreach)  

**Target sponsors:**
- Local/regional brands aligned with independent entertainment, music, arts, current events
- Service businesses targeting the same demographic as BlackMarker.tv viewers
- Brands that have already advertised on similar independent platforms

**Starter sponsorship package:**
- Opening/closing billboard on all shows for the month
- 1 host-read integration per week on flagship show
- Social media brand mentions
- **Price: $500–$1,500/month**

This is not the final price — it's the relationship-building rate that creates case studies for larger deals.

**Success metric:** 2–3 sponsor conversations had. First sponsor signed by Day 90.
