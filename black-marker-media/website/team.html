Our Story

Built From the Ground Up.
Intentionally.

Black Marker Media didn't start as a software company trying to enter streaming. It started as a live event production team that realized every client they served needed more than a great show — they needed a platform to build an audience that would come back.

That insight became the foundation of everything. The BAM™ formula — Build, Amplify, Monetize — is the framework that emerged from producing hundreds of live events and watching creators struggle to convert one-time audiences into recurring revenue.

BlackMarker.TV was built first — as the proof of concept. Before we sold a single ChannelCast subscription or production retainer, we built and operated the network ourselves. Every system we offer clients is the same system we run.

Today, Black Marker Media operates three connected divisions that create a compounding loop — each leg strengthening the others, and each client relationship feeding the entire ecosystem.

2022
Black Marker Media LLC established in Wesley Chapel, FL. Live production and streaming services launched.
2023
BlackMarker.TV launched as the company's independent live broadcast network — home to original programming, PPV events, and the first proof of the BAM™ formula in action.
2025
ChannelCast productized as a standalone managed platform service — bringing the same infrastructure powering BlackMarker.TV to external clients.
2026
Phase 1 growth plan activated — six-figure capital deployed across all three divisions with Year 1 ARR target of $270K–$432K.
Next
Active partner and investor conversations underway. Three-division expansion in progress across platform clients, production retainers, and network programming.
