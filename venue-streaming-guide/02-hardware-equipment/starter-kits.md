## Notes on All Kits

- **Budget does not include installation labor.** Professional AV installation typically runs $75-150/hour.
- **Prices are approximate** and based on early 2026 US retail. Check current pricing before purchasing.
- **Start with the starter kit** and upgrade components as your streaming program matures. A $3,000 kit that gets used every week produces more value than a $30,000 kit that sits idle.
- **Always budget 10-15% extra** for cables, adapters, and small accessories that add up.
